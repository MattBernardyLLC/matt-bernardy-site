MY APPROACH

Building Marketing Engines in 6 Months

Most startups ready to scale have junior marketers executing tactics without data to guide them. The result? Random acts of marketing that burn budget without moving metrics. My approach is different: I start with customer research and market data, build measurement frameworks first, then construct teams and systems around what the data tells us will work. Every phase is testable, measurable, and grounded in evidence.

Market Strategy

Phase 1: Customer Discovery

Conduct customer conversations and attend industry events. Deliver market readout grounded in real insight.

Phase 2: Experimentation

Test messaging, channels, tactics. Measure outcomes consistently with focus on pipeline.

Phase 3: Scale What Works

Build cohesive plan around proven tactics. Create compelling narrative and measurement frameworks.

Team & Systems

Phase 1: Company Discovery

Dive into GTM data, product analytics, competitive landscape. Interview internal team.

Phase 2: Operational Rhythm

Establish 1:1s, business meetings, AI innovation discussions. Define culture and expectations.

Phase 3: Hire the Team

Build capacity to create, distribute, and measure. Hire for strategic problems, leverage AI for repeatable processes.
